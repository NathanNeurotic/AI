Summary

Adobe Firefly is a family of creative generative AI models deeply integrated into Adobe's Creative Cloud suite. Its primary advantage is its commercial safety, as the model is trained exclusively on licensed and public domain content, providing users with legal indemnification. Google Veo

Summary

Veo is Google DeepMind's state-of-the-art video generation model, targeting filmmakers and professionals who need hyper-realistic, cinematic quality. The latest version, Veo 3, introduces groundbreaking native audio generation (dialogue, sound effects) and supports up to 4K resolution. It offers extensive creative controls for camera movement and character consistency.[5, 12, 1, 13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 18]

This service is from Google and is unrelated to `veo.co`, which sells AI cameras for sports.[19, 20]

Pricing / Tiers

Access is available through multiple channels with different pricing structures:

  • API Access: Veo 2 costs $0.50 per second of generated video.[15] Veo 3 costs $0.75 per second for video and audio output.[14]
  • Google AI Subscriptions: The Google AI Pro plan ($19.99/month) includes Veo 2 access.[1, 16] The Google AI Ultra plan ($249.99/month) offers the highest limits and early access to Veo 3.[16]
  • Third-Party Platforms: Services like `veo3.ai` offer credit-based plans starting at $37.50/month (annual billing).[17]

Hunyuan Video (Tencent)

Summary

Hunyuan Video is a powerful, open-source video foundation model from Chinese tech giant Tencent. With 13 billion parameters, it is positioned as a leading open-source alternative to closed models like Sora, providing developers and researchers with a high-performance tool for generating HD video up to 720p.[21, 53, 54]

Pricing / Tiers

As an open-source model, Hunyuan is free to download and use for those with the necessary hardware (80GB GPU recommended).[54] For others, third-party platforms offer pay-per-use access:

  • Platform Access (e.g., hunyuanvideoai.com): 12 credits per 5-second video.[53]
  • API Access (e.g., Fal.ai): Approximately $0.40 per video generation.[55]

Seedance (ByteDance)

Summary

Seedance is a high-performance video foundation model from ByteDance (parent company of TikTok). It competes with top-tier models like Sora and Veo, excelling at creating coherent, multi-shot narrative videos with strong prompt adherence and cinematic aesthetics. It is engineered for both high quality and inference efficiency.[102, 104, 2, 21, 105, 106, 107, 108, 109, 110, 26, 27, 111, 112, 113, 114, 115, 116]

Pricing / Tiers

Pricing is primarily API-focused and highly cost-competitive. It is based on a pay-as-you-go token system via BytePlus ModelArk.[104, 110]

  • Seedance 1.0 Pro: $2.50 per million output tokens. (A 5s, 1080p video costs ~$0.61).[104]
  • Seedance 1.0 Lite: $1.80 per million output tokens. (A 5s, 480p video costs ~$0.12).[104]
  • Other analyses place a 5s HD render at approximately $0.50.[102, 109]
